01 / Who we are

What is AMBIO-N in few words

A brand new experience for sourcing needs across industry for organisations seeking alternative sustainable solutions.

AMBIO-N is a materials and product search market platform connecting Buyers to Suppliers procuring materials, product and equipment to aid with a circular value chain.

The platform will provide thought leadership on the subject of sustainability - what it can mean for your business and the value it can bring to your organisation.

Our Vision

We want to increase awareness of what sustainability means for business and inspire people to make positive changes towards a more responsible value chain.

Our Mission

To connect Buyers and Sellers in industry who are aligned with an interest in advancing towards a more circular operational business model.
